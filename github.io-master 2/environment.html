The Environment

Every year, 14 BILLION pounds of trash are dumped in the ocean. This trash can be chemicals, plastic, waste, metal, it can be anything! It clogs beatiful beaches, then goes on to hurt many, including sealife. Harmless creatures such as the turtle below, get trapped in these horrible plastic nets. Tons more animals also get stuck in plastic six-pack rings, and chemicals can damage their internal systems.

But that's not all! Pollution from chemical and smog have a lot of bad effects too. All this smog is harmful to our health, and can even cause asthma!

Why should we take care of the environment?

So why should WE take care of the environment? Here are some reasons why:

  1. Taking care of the environment can help improve our health
  2. Taking care of the environment now can help ensure that the earth will be healthy for years to come.
  3. Taking care of the environment can help make sure species don't go extinct!

So how can we help take care of the environment?

There are many ways we can take care of the environment. Some include:

  • Recycle paper, plastic, boxes, or anything whenever possible
  • Bring reusable bags to the grocery store instead of plastic ones
  • Always use reusable items and limit use of plastic
  • Volunteer to help pick up trash around your neighborhood!
  • Don't litter
  • Help spread awareness!
